Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 7, 2025

Evoke ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: When you sign up via Facebook or email, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Voice Recordings: When you create posts using voice input, your audio is processed to generate text. We do not store raw audio recordings.
  • Generated Content: Posts you create, including enhanced text and generated graphics.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process your voice input and generate enhanced posts
  • Create personalised graphics for your content
  • Communicate with you about updates and features
  • Ensure the security of our services

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate Evoke:

  • Supabase: For authentication and data storage
  • Facebook: For social login (if you choose to sign in with Facebook)
  • AI Services: To enhance your text and generate graphics

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, alteration, or destruction.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your data at any time by visiting our Data Deletion page or contacting us directly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
