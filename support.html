Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my first post?

Simply tap the microphone button and start talking about a show or movie you want to recommend. Evoke will transcribe your voice, enhance your words, and generate a matching graphic automatically.

What happens during my free trial?

You get 3 days of unlimited access to all Evoke features. Create as many posts as you like. After the trial, you can continue with a subscription at £6.99/month.

How do I share my posts?

After creating a post, tap the share button to copy the text and image to your clipboard, or share directly to Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and other platforms.

Can I edit my posts after creating them?

Yes! You can re-record your voice to regenerate the post, or manually edit the text before sharing.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime from your account settings. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use industry-standard encryption and secure cloud storage. We never sell your data. See our Privacy Policy for details.
