Data Deletion

At Evoke, we respect your right to control your personal data. You can request deletion of your data at any time using the methods described below.

What Data We Delete

When you request data deletion, we will remove:

  1. Your account information (name, email, profile picture)
  2. All posts you've created (text and generated graphics)
  3. Your preferences and settings
  4. Any associated authentication data

How to Request Data Deletion

Option 1: Email Request

Send an email to privacy@getevoke.co.uk with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include:

  1. The email address associated with your Evoke account
  2. Confirmation that you want all your data deleted

We will process your request within 30 days and send confirmation when complete.

Option 2: In-App Deletion

If you have access to the app:

  1. Open Evoke and go to Settings
  2. Tap "Account"
  3. Tap "Delete My Account"
  4. Confirm deletion

Your data will be permanently deleted immediately.

Facebook Users

If you signed up using Facebook Login, you can also manage your data connection through Facebook:

  1. Go to your Facebook Settings
  2. Navigate to "Apps and Websites"
  3. Find Evoke and click "Remove"
  4. Select "Delete all posts, photos, and videos" if prompted

After removing the app from Facebook, please also contact us to ensure complete deletion of your data from our systems.
